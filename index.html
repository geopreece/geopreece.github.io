ABOUT ME


Having graduated in the summer of 2020 into a less than ideal job market, I thought what better time to do something positive. I started volunteering at my local Cancer Research UK shop where I took upon a range of tasks and have had a great experience- and importantly, a laugh!

I continue to volunteer at the shop (when possible) two days a week, doing things such as:

  • Serving customers
  • Making sales
  • Taking donations
  • Exchanging tender at the bank
  • Making the shop COVID safe, and most of all
  • Preventing kids on their dad’s shoulders from hitting the ceiling fan

I am into pretty much all sports but I enjoy watching and taking part in rugby, skiing and football the most. I snapped my ACL when I was 16 and haven’t got back into rugby and football so much unfortunately but I still love watching. I watch more football now, purely because the news and broadcastings are more accessible, so it is more convenient being disappointed by Manchester United than Sale Sharks!

Over the past few years my main form of exercise has been going to the gym. The gym provides a bit of time in the day to listen to some music or a podcast while working out. I like biomechanics and anatomy so figuring out the right exercises in a session to maximise the mechanical stress on the muscles is fun. Despite appearances, or lack thereof, I train for bodybuilding as I find the most enjoyment and it is easier to train for than Olympic lifting, powerlifting and CrossFit. I also try to improve my mobility and functionality so I can train injury free and without pain!
